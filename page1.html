Renzo Castagnino

Data Scientist - The George Washington University

In the following graphs, we will compare the educational and healthcare expediture, to the overall military expediture from the year 2000 to 2018. Lets begin by showing in the map the total expediture eductional expediture by country.

MEAN OF EDUCATION EXPENDITURE BY COUNTRY FROM 2000 to 2018 ( BILLION OF USD)

Education vs Military Expenditure in 2018

As we can observe from the bar chart, the country with the expediture not only in eduation, but also in education, is the United States with almost 1000 billions of USD in education, compared with a 648 billion of usd in military expediture. Furthermore, all of the countries has more expenditure in education than in military except for China and Saudi Arabia. both countries expend more in military than education.

Education vs Military Expenses from 2000 to 2018

There is an overall upward trend from 2000 to 2018 in both military and education expenses. However, we can see that for all the years, education has remained higher that the military expediture.
